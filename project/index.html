Noises Around You

-- an R Shiny app development project on Open Data NYC

We know a lot of New Yorkers are as picky as we are, especially when it comes to renting/buying an apartment. New York City is such a big apple glazed with hustle and bustle, which not only gives you convenience of living but also annoys you with its side effects, such as noises, rodents, and bugs.

This project explores and visualizes the noise level in New York City by integrating analyses of the 311 complaints data in 2015 on NYC Open Data Portal, the geographical data of construction sites, fire stations, hospitals, and clubs in NYC. The R Shiny App aim to help users navigate through our findings from 3 different perspectives: Statistics, map visualization and data tables.
